Corned Beef Recipe

Description

Corned beef is a classic Filipino breakfast dish that is cooked with onions, best served with steamed rice and a fried egg!

Ingredients

  • 1 of any canned corned beef
  • 1 small onion, cut in rings
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 4 tsp cooking oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Put oil in pan and wait for it to heat up. Place onions in pan and saute for about 3 minutes, stirring to prevent burning.
  2. Add garli and saute for about 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  3. Throw in the corned beef and mix with everything, stirring for 5 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
