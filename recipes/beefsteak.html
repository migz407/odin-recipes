Beefsteak Recipe

Description

Beefsteak is a classic Filipino dish made with thinly sliced beef braised in a mixture of soy sauce, onions, and citrus!

This dish is heary, tasty, and pairs excellent with steamed white rice!

Ingredients

  • 1.5-2 lbs beef sirloin thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice or calamansi juice
  • 5 cloves of garlic (or however much you want!)
  • 1-2 whole onion (any kind will do; white, yellow, or red;cut in circles)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1-2 pinches of salt
  • 4 tblspn cooking oil
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Marinate beef in soy sauce, lemon (or calamansi), and ground pepper for at least 30 min-1 hour. Overnight for best result!
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a pan, then fry half of the onions until they become soft and transluscent. Set aside.
  3. Drain and set aside for later the marinade from the beef. Fry the beef on the same pan 1 minute per side.
  4. Add more oil if needed. Saute garlic for 30 seconds, then pour remaining marinade and water. Bring to boil.
  5. Add beef and cooked onions. Also add rest of raw onions and simmer until meat is tender.
  6. Season with more black pepper and salt if needed.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
