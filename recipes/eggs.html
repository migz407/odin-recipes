Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

Scrambled eggs is a classic American breakfast item that pairs well with almost anything!

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • 1-2 pinches salt and pepper
  • 3-4 tblspn butter

Steps

  1. Crack open eggs and whisk in a bowl until the egg whites are thoroughly mixed in with the yolk.
  2. Heat up pan and place butter in pan until its melted.
  3. Once butter is melted, add your whisked eggs to the pan.
  4. Let it cook while moving eggs softly in a circular motion to creat fluffy texture.
  5. Once all the wet and raw eggs are cooked, serve on a plate immediately and enjoy!
